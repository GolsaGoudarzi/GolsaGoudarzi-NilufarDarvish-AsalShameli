Part I: Historical Evolution of Cosmological Models

  • Ptolemy and Geocentrism: Dominated for centuries, placing a stationary Earth at the center.
  • Copernicus and Heliocentrism: Shifted the center to the Sun, elegantly solving retrograde motion without epicycles.
  • GalileoвҖҷs Evidence: Discovered Jupiter's moons and Venus's phases, proving not everything orbits Earth.
  • KeplerвҖҷs Laws: Described mathematically how planets follow elliptical paths, though the cause remained unknown.
